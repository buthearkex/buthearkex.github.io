Our Product

Our real time image analysis provides you with valuable information, such as:

Age

We accurately estimate the age of people and also provide you with a range of variation.

Gender

We are able to determine the gender of people very accurately.

Emotions

We are able to track each persons emotional state and place them on a scale for six different emotions.

Racial classification

We are able to classify people based on their ethnicity.

Automatically generated reports

Create personalized reports based on your own needs. To make educated decisions, reports can also use data from other softwares you're using.

Easy to install

You can use your existing camera hardware, or we provide you with all the hardware you need!
